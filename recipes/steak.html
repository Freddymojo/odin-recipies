Ribeye Steak Recipe

Pan Frying a Juicey Ribeye Steak

The method I typically use to pan fry a steak. A simple recipe, but a good ribeye doesnt need much help.

Results in a medium steak.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. Rub steak generously with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan on medium high
  3. Add oil once heated
  4. Add butter
  5. Place steak into center of pan and let sit for 5 minutes
  6. Flip steak and heat for 5 minutes, spooning melted butter onto the crust from the pan
  7. Remove steak from pan and check internal temp for safety
  8. Enjoy!
