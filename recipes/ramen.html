Korean Ramen recipe

A buddy of mine told me this was a thing and I didn't believe him until I looked it up

Try some cheesey ramen if you want. Or don't

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of Shin Ramen
  • 1 Whole egg
  • 1 slice american cheese
  • Few handfulls of chopped green onion
  • 1.25 cups water

Directions

  1. Place in 1.25 cups of water into a frying pan.
  2. Bring it up to a boil.
  3. Once the water is boiling, add in the dried ramen noodles.
  4. Then, add in the dried veggies packet.
  5. Next, add-in only Â½ the Spicy Seasoning Packet
  6. Let the noodles boil until it starts to loosen. Use your chopsticks to loosen the noodles. Then create a small circle in the middle of the pan.
  7. Drop-in an egg into the middle of the pan.
  8. Place lid on. Reduce heat to a LOW. Let it cook for 1 minute
  9. After 1 minute, lift the lid. Place 1 slice of American Cheese and add-on the chopped green onions.
  10. Take it off the heat. Place it on the table and eat
