Chocolate Chip Cookies

A recipe to create chocolate chip cookies

Warm chocolate chip cookies perfect for the holidays

Treat your family this holiday season

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups simisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Cream together the butter white sugar and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time then stir the vanilla. Dissolve the baking soda in water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips. Drop but large spoonfuls into ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned
